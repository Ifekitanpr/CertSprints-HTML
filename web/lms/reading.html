INTRODUCTION

Introduction to ACMP, the Standard for Change Management and Code of Ethics

Imagine having a trusted guide to navigate the complex world of organizational change—that’s exactly what the Association of Change Management Professionals, the ACMP Standard for Change Management, and the ACMP Code of Ethics provide.

ACMP sets the global foundation for change management excellence, transforming a once fragmented practice into a structured, recognized profession. Its Standard serves as your end-to-end playbook—defining how to assess, design, implement, and sustain change—while the Code of Ethics ensures that change is led with integrity, transparency, and respect for people.

By understanding ACMP’s framework and principles, you gain more than knowledge—you gain clarity, credibility, and the ability to drive change that actually works. It becomes your roadmap for turning strategy into adoption, ensuring that change is not just delivered, but realized, sustained, and trusted across the organization.
