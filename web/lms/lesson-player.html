Lesson Overview

Welcome to Module 3. After completing the change management roadmap in Module 2, you are now ready to explore the first and most critical process group in the ACMP Standard: Evaluate Change Impact and Organizational Readiness.

In this module, you will examine how a proposed change affects the organization and determine whether the organization is truly prepared to adopt it. Many initiatives fail not because the solution is flawed, but because the organization is not ready.

Core Concepts

Change Definition Case for Change Change Impact Assessment Organizational Readiness Sponsor Identification Stakeholder Analysis Risk Assessment Change Adoption

What you will learn

Define a change at the behavioral level — not just the technical one
Develop and articulate a clear vision of the future state
Identify and segment stakeholders by impact, not by title
Assess readiness across willingness, ability, capacity, and culture
Evaluate leadership alignment as a leading indicator of adoption success
Surface and prioritize risks before they become execution problems
Apply the 16 structured diagnostic processes within this process group
