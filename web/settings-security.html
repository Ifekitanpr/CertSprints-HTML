Sign out of device

This will immediately log you out of your account on all devices and browsers, except for the one you are currently using. Use this if:

  • You signed in on a public or shared computer.
  • You suspect someone else might have access to your account.
  • You just want to refresh your security.
  • Note: You'll need to re-enter your password on those devices to log back in.
